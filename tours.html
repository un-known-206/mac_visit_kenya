Madikwe-reserve

Madikwe-reserve is located in the SouthAfrica mostly in the northern parts. The reserve is home to a rich variety of wildlife, including the Big 5: lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo, and both black and white rhinos. Additionally, visitors can encounter over 120 species of mammals and more than 350 bird species, making it a paradise for wildlife enthusiasts and photographers.
